Gesture Guide

  • Right Hand Pinch - Pinch your thumb and index finger to control sphere size (closer = smaller, further = larger)
  • Right Hand Rotation - Rotate your right hand to control the sphere's rotation
  • Left Hand Point - Touch the sphere with your left index finger to change its color
  • Webcam Feed - Drag the camera feed box to reposition it on screen
  • Hand Tracking - Both hands are tracked in 3D space with visible landmarks
